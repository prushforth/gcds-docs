## Problems maps can solve

Maps have a wide variety of uses, including:

- basemap display of a region
- locations and location properties of interest
- thematic maps
- time series maps
- spatial distribution or location of resources

## Related components

- <a href="{{ links.mapLayer }}">Layer</a> is exclusively used to add map content.
